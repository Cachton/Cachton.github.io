La maison à Prevost

La maison à Prevost is a project I made with a team in a video class. We had to create a short film from scratch. The video was filmed in a house in the small town of Prevost and in a parking close to the school. To get the right lighting we filmed most of the footage at night which proved to be a real fight against drowsiness. The final cut was edited on Davinci Resolve.
