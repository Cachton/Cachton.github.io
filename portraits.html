Portraits

Portraits is a project I made in a photoshop class. We were taught the basics of photoshop and this was the first project that we creatively got to apply what we had learned. The assignment was to make four cards that represented us so that we would get to know the class. I decided to use the “missing texture” pattern from Gmod because I grew up on video games and I selected four of my favorite artists that are inspirations to me.
